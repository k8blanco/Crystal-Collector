Crystal Collector

  • At the beginning of the game, you will be given a random number.
  • Each of the four crystals is worth a different number, which will be hidden until you click on it. Each time you click on a crystal, that number will be added to your total score.
  • You win by matching your total score to the given number. You will lose if your number exceeds the given number.
  • Hint: Each time the game starts, the crystals will be assigned a new number!
