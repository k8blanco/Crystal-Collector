Crystal Collector

  1. At the beginning of the game, you will be given a random number.
  2. Each of the four crystals is worth a different (hidden) number.
  3. Each time you click a crystal, that number will be added to your total score.
  4. You win by matching your total score to the given number. You will lose if your number exceeds the given number.

    5. Hint: Each time the game starts, the crystals will be assigned a new number!
